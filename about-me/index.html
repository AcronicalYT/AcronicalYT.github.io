About Me:

Here's a little bit about me, my name is Oliver and I love making content for Twitch and YouTube, it has been a dream of mine to have my own YouTube channel since I found out about it. I also love to program and make my own projects whenever I feel like it; this could be entirely my idea, or it could be following online tutorials that I find helpful. Generally people do this 'career' for fame or money, but I want to do it for entertainment of myself and other people, and I hope that I can inspire other people to start this pathway themselves. Thank you for checking out my website and hopefully you will support me on my journey up through the ranks of content creation.

This has been a little bit about me and I hope you enjoy/continue to enjoy my content!
