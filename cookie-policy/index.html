Cookies...

I'm sure you've all heard of cookies, those things that larger companies put on your device so they know what you did at 08:47 last thursday morning. But how do I use them?

Simply put, I collect absolutely 0 data from you. The cookies on this site are added on by external services like YouTube (Google), Twitch, and GitHub.

These sites add cookies because of embeds, or analytical reasons. I have no access to this data, and each site has their own cookie policy that you can go and read. The only cookies I use on this site are for the projects page to locally store the creator you've selected out of the preferences - I also do not see any of this.
